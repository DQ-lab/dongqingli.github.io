Professional Experience

I currently serve as a Lead Data Scientist at Alibaba Cloud in Shanghai, leading the development of a thousand-GPU AI and Large Language Model (LLM) Platform for large government clients, including Fudan University and the Shanghai AI Lab. Prior to this role, I was the data engineer in E-commerce business, mainly responsible for the user growth in AliExpress.

Before joining Alibaba Inc., I worked as an actuary in statistical pricing projects, gaining valuable experience in the insurance consulting sector in Sydney.
